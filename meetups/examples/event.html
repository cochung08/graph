Dayhike - Incline training on Toms' Thumb via Gateway Trailhead

2
  • Il y a 2 jours · 06:00
  • Gateway Area Trailhead (McDowells)

I need to get in some incline training and what better way to do it than with hiking buddies?

This 11.2 mile hike has 2488ft Average Elevation Gain over 5.6 miles. It's basically up up up and then down on the way out. Great hike, and the trailhead has fantastic facilities. Since it's a straight yo yo hike you can always turn around at any time if the going gets too tough. Excellent training.
